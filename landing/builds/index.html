ServiceMedia was originally developed to support the Unity game engine, and scenes may be configured for apps on iOS, Android or PC builds. The ServiceMedia app consolidates all the network domains into one app for demostration purposes. When the app launches, you first select a world to explore - that is, an interlinked collection of immersive scenes (an immersive scene is to a "world" as a webpage is to a "site"). WebXR versions of each scene are also available under their respective domain names, and these versions (web and app) share data and network connections, and can link between each other.

  • RealityMangler - demos and experimental weirdness
  • Philosophers' Garden - landscapes of consciousness
  • Eloquent Noise - virtual words
  • theKorkus - psychoactive garage rock
  • 3D Case Files - immersive details
  • The Family Share - cherished memories
  • Snakewater - tentacular rock opera
  • SpaceTime RailRoad - quantum traveling
  • VisibleCity - city views and data
  • ValueBring - future shops
with more to come!
